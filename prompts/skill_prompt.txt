# 2. Logic: Status Effect Terminologies (Intensity vs. Stacks)
You must distinguish between the "Power" of an effect and its "Duration/Count":

1. **Count (层数/回数)**: 
   - Keywords: `Count`, `回数`, `횟수`, `stacks`.
   - Action: Translate as "**层数**" (or "**次数**" for actions).
   - *Ex*: `[Burn] Count +2` -> `[Burn]层数增加2`.

2. **Potency (强度/等级)**:
   - Keywords: No keywords, just a number; or `Potency`, `付与`, `부여`.
   - Action: Translate as "**级...强度**" or "**点...强度**".
   - *Ex*: `Inflict 5 Burn` -> `对目标施加5级烧伤强度`.
   - *Ex*: `Apply 3 [Sinking]` -> `对目标施加3级[Sinking]强度`.

# 3. Terminology Mapping (Standardized)
Use these exact terms for consistency:
- Inflict/Apply/Gain: 施加 / 获得
- At the start of the combat/turn: 战斗/回合开始时
- On hit: 命中时
- Burst (Tremor): 引爆 (振动)
- Spend/Consume: 消耗
- Target: 目标
- Self: 自身
